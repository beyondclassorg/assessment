Beyond Class · Identity & Impact Assessment

There are no
wrong answers here.

This assessment is designed to help you discover who you are — not test what you know. You don't need to have your life figured out. You don't need to know what career you want. Just answer honestly with the first thing that feels true for you right now.

💡
Go with your gut. Your first instinct is usually the most honest answer. Don't overthink it.
🙅
There's no right answer. This isn't a test. Every option is equally valid — they just reveal different things about who you are.
🌱
You're still becoming. Some questions ask about the future. It's okay to imagine. Think about what excites or bothers you most right now.
🤝
Ask a parent or adult if you get stuck. Some questions are big. Talking them through out loud is totally fine.
